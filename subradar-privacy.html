Subradax Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Data Storage

Subradax stores all your subscription and renewal data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No email addresses or contact details
  • No financial account credentials
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking data

Local Data

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Subscription names you enter
  • Renewal dates and amounts you input
  • App settings and preferences

This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any external server.

Notifications

Subradax uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming renewals. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not require network connectivity.

In-App Purchases

Subradax Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment and subscription management. We do not have access to your payment details.

Third-Party Services

Subradax does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Subradax is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes
