SubRadar Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

SubRadar ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SubRadar app.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

When you use SubRadar, you may provide the following information:

  • Subscription Data: Names, amounts, currencies, billing cycles, and payment dates of your subscriptions (stored locally on your device)
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications, we store reminder preferences locally
  • Feedback: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and message content

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal identifiers (name, email, phone number) by default
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do NOT use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

How We Use Your Information

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device and is used solely for:

  • Displaying your subscription list and spending overview
  • Sending local notifications for upcoming payments
  • Exporting data when you choose to do so

Optional Cloud Sync

SubRadar offers an optional cloud sync feature that requires you to set up your own self-hosted SubRadar API server. If you choose to use this feature:

  • Your subscription data will be transmitted to your own server
  • We do NOT have access to your self-hosted server or the data stored there
  • You are responsible for securing your own server

Data Storage

  • Default: All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Optional: If you enable cloud sync with your own server, data is stored on your server
  • We do NOT store your data on any servers we control

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

SubRadar is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device as long as you keep the app installed. You can delete all data by:

  • Deleting individual subscriptions within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device (all local data will be removed)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
