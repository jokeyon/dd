Frequently Asked Questions

What is SubRadar?

SubRadar is a subscription and renewal tracker that helps you remember when your recurring payments are due. It stores all data locally on your device.

Do I need to create an account?

No. SubRadar works entirely offline. All your data stays on your device - no login required.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free version: Track up to 3 subscription items.
SubRadar Premium: Unlimited items + export/share feature.

How do notifications work?

SubRadar sends local reminders before your renewal dates. Make sure you've enabled notifications in iOS Settings → SubRadar.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SubRadar Premium → Cancel Subscription.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device only. We never upload or access your information.
